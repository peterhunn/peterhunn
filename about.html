Variant is a first-check crypto venture capital firm. We lead pre-seed and seed rounds in the most ambitious teams building the ownership economy.

The Ownership Economy

Variant was founded on a simple idea: the networks that define the next generation of the internet will turn users into owners. We call this the Ownership Economy — a model in which users of a product or service become stakeholders, participating in its growth and governance.

Crypto enables this shift. Through tokens, NFTs, and on-chain governance, the projects we back can align the incentives of users, developers, and investors in ways that were previously impossible. When users own a piece of what they help build, they become co-creators — and the products they build together become more valuable and more defensible than anything a traditional company could create alone.

We believe this model will win across virtually every sector: finance, social networks, creative tools, infrastructure, gaming, and more. The companies that figure out how to turn their users into owners will define the next era of the internet.

What We Do

We lead pre-seed and seed rounds for the most ambitious crypto founders. We write checks typically ranging from $500K to $5M, and we take a hands-on role in helping companies succeed. Beyond capital, we offer founders access to our platform — a dedicated team with expertise in regulatory strategy, go-to-market, mechanism design, and talent acquisition.

We also run selective follow-on positions in later rounds when a company is central to our thesis. Our portfolio spans DeFi, infrastructure, consumer crypto, NFTs, DAOs, and beyond.

We are the only early-stage crypto fund with a full-time platform team dedicated exclusively to helping portfolio companies succeed. This is a core part of what makes Variant different.

Our Approach

We build in public. We write extensively about crypto, share our research freely, and engage openly with the ecosystem. We believe that the best founders want investors who are genuine participants in the space — not just capital providers.

We take the long view. Crypto markets are volatile, but the underlying technology and its potential for transforming human coordination are not. We invest with a multi-year time horizon and support founders through every market cycle.

We invest in people as much as in ideas. The ownership economy is still early, and the eventual winners will be teams that can adapt, learn, and execute through uncertainty. We look for founders with exceptional conviction, intellectual rigor, and the resilience to build through difficult conditions.

Fund History

Fund I — 2020

Variant launched in 2020, with Jesse Walden, Spencer Noon, and Li Jin as founding partners. The fund focused on early-stage crypto companies building at the frontier of the ownership economy, making initial investments in projects like Uniswap, Phantom, Mirror, and Flashbots.

Fund II — $110M, 2021

Variant closed its second fund at $110M, cementing the ownership economy as a defining investment thesis for the crypto venture space. Li Jin's Atelier Ventures merged into Variant as part of this raise, expanding the team's focus to include creator economy applications of crypto.

Fund III — $450M, 2022

Variant raised $450M across two vehicles: a $150M seed fund and a $300M opportunity fund for follow-on investments in the best-performing companies. This was one of the largest crypto seed funds raised, reflecting strong conviction in the long-term trajectory of the ownership economy despite market volatility.

Fund IV — ~$250M target, 2025

Variant filed with the SEC in September 2025 to raise a fourth flagship fund targeting approximately $250M, continuing its focus on pre-seed and seed investments in crypto's most ambitious founding teams.
