Working at Variant

Variant is a small, focused firm. We don't hire often, but when we do, we look for people who are genuinely passionate about crypto — not just professionally, but personally. The best team members are people who would be participating in this ecosystem whether or not it was their job.

We value intellectual curiosity, rigorous thinking, and a bias toward action. We work hard and expect a lot from ourselves and each other, but we also believe in sustainable pace and genuine ownership of your work.

If you're interested in working at Variant and don't see a role that fits below, feel free to reach out. We're always open to meeting exceptional people.
